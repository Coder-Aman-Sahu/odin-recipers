Fried Rice

Description

Making fried rice is a breeze with the recipe you'll find below. The process is simple — preheat your pan before swirling in oil and aromatics, then add uncooked proteins if applicable. Add in rice and vegetables, vigorously tossing ingredients for that toasty, golden-brown color that signals big flavor.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Pour in vegetable oil. Add garlic and stir until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Add carrots and peas; cook and stir until vegetables begin to soften, 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Move vegetables to the side of the skillet and pour beaten eggs onto the other side. Scramble eggs until solid and broken into pieces. Mix eggs with the vegetables.
  3. Stir rice into vegetable and egg mixture; add soy sauce and sesame oil. Cook and stir rice mixture until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes.
